# Appointment Scheduler

Welcome! This chatbot helps you book banking appointments quickly and easily.

**Available consultations:**
- Investing
- Mortgage
- Pension

**Meeting formats:** Branch Meeting, Online Meeting, or Phone

**Try it:** *"I'd like to discuss my mortgage at the branch next Tuesday afternoon."*

---

Use the **settings gear icon** to explore how the agent works:

- **Smart availability search** — when your requested time is fully booked, the
  assistant automatically searches nearby days and offers the closest
  alternatives (on by default).
- **Show agent activity** — watch the agent's tool calls and search strategy as
  expandable steps in the chat, from a short summary to full debug detail.
- **NLP experiment toggles** — optionally route datetime/entity understanding
  through a separate NLP pipeline (research feature from the accompanying study).
